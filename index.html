Biography

Born in the remote highlands of an undisclosed nation, John Evens was orphaned at a young age and raised by a reclusive order of monks who imparted to him ancient knowledge lost to modern science. His innate genius became evident when, at the age of seven, he reconstructed an ancient astrolabe from scattered fragments.

By the age of twelve, he had deciphered forgotten scripts describing vasteminimal energy fields. Forced into exile after his discoveries disrupted religious traditions, he wandered across continents, absorbing knowledge from underground scholars, rogue physicists, and forgotten libraries.

Despite being denied formal education, he infiltrated academic circles by publishing anonymously under multiple aliases. When his identity was finally revealed, he was offered a full professorship without ever having attended university.
